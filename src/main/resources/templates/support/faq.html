Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ShopWave.

🛒 Orders & Delivery

You can track your order by visiting the Track My Order page and entering your order number. You'll find your order number in the confirmation email or in your order history.
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities usually receive orders within 2–3 days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pin codes.
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be modified. Contact us at support@shopwave.com for urgent cancellation requests.
We currently deliver to over 20,000 pin codes across India. Enter your pin code at checkout to confirm delivery availability for your area.

💳 Payments

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Net Banking, and Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay). All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Yes. We use industry-standard PCI-DSS compliant payment processing. Your card details are never stored on our servers — they are encrypted and processed by our payment gateway directly.
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and verify the returned item. The amount is credited back to your original payment method. UPI/bank transfers may take an additional 2–3 days.

📦 Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt. Visit our Returns page to initiate a return.
Yes! Exchanges are available for size or colour variants of the same product, subject to availability. Contact our support team within 30 days of delivery to arrange an exchange.
